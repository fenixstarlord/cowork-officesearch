## Target Area

## Key Corridors

### East Side
- **SE Powell Blvd** -- Major arterial. Commercial zoning both sides. Mixed-use from SE 12th to SE 52nd.
- **SE Division St** -- Heavily developed with new mixed-use construction.
- **SE Hawthorne Blvd** -- Established commercial corridor. Apartments above storefronts.
- **SE Belmont St** -- Parallel to Hawthorne, mixed retail/residential.
- **SE Clinton St** -- Quieter bike corridor parallel to Division.
- **SE Milwaukie Ave** -- Orange Line corridor, new development.
- **NE Broadway / NE Weidler** -- Commercial corridor through inner NE.
- **NE/SE Sandy Blvd** -- Diagonal arterial, commercial zoning.

### West Side
- **NW 23rd Ave** -- Retail/restaurant corridor, apartments above.
- **W Burnside** -- Divides NW/SW, commercial zoning.
- **SW Macadam Ave** -- South Waterfront development corridor.

## Transit Notes

- **MAX Light Rail**: Blue, Red, Green, Yellow, Orange lines serve Downtown, Pearl, Lloyd, Hollywood, SE
- **MAX Orange Line**: Milwaukie corridor — stations at SE 17th/Holgate, SE Rhine, through Brooklyn/Westmoreland/Sellwood
- **TriMet bus lines**: Powell (9), Division (4), Hawthorne (14), Belmont (15), Broadway (77), Sandy (12)
- **Portland Streetcar**: NS Line (Pearl to South Waterfront), A/B Loop (Lloyd to PSU)
- Walk Score 80-98 in central neighborhoods
